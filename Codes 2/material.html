Cotton Fabric

Cotton is a soft, breathable fabric from cotton plant fibers, known for its comfort and versatility. It's commonly used in clothing and home textiles due to its absorbency and durability.

Silk Fabric

Silk is a luxurious natural fiber known for its smooth texture and sheen, produced mainly by silkworms. Valued for its elegance, silk is popular in clothing, bedding etc.

Linen Fabric

Linen is a durable, breathable fabric made from flax fibers. Known for its crisp texture, it becomes softer with washing and excels in hot weather due to its moisture.
