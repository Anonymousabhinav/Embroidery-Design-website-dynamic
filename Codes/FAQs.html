Schiffli Embroidery FAQs

  1. What is Schiffli embroidery?

    Schiffli embroidery is a type of machine embroidery that uses a special machine resembling a loom, capable of creating intricate, lace-like patterns on a large scale.

  2. How do I choose a design for my project?

    Choosing a design involves considering the fabric, the intended use of the item, and aesthetic preferences to ensure the design complements the product effectively.

  3. What file formats do you accept for custom designs?

    We accept various file formats including DST, PES, EXP, and others. It's best to provide artwork in a vector format for precision.

  4. Do you offer embroidery services for bulk orders?

    Yes, we provide services for bulk orders, offering scalable solutions for large quantities with optimized production timelines and pricing.

  5. Can I use my own fabric for custom designs?

    Absolutely! You can provide your own fabric for custom embroidery projects. Please ensure it meets our quality standards for the best results.

  6. How long does it take to complete an embroidery order?

    The completion time can vary based on the complexity and size of the order but generally takes between 2 to 4 weeks.

  7. What are the pricing options for different design sizes?

    Pricing varies by design complexity, size, and the number of stitches required. We offer quotes based on your specific requirements.

  8. Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we offer international shipping options. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

  9. What is the maximum size you can embroider?

    The maximum embroidery size is generally determined by the capabilities of our machines, which can handle up to 1.5 meters in width.

Embroidery Design FAQs

  1. What is the difference between Schiffli embroidery and other machine embroidery techniques?

    Schiffli embroidery is unique for its ability to produce large-scale, intricate designs more efficiently, suited for mass production of lace and textile embellishments.

  2. Can you create custom embroidery designs from a sketch or image?

    Yes, we can transform sketches or images into embroidery-ready designs. We work with you to refine the details to suit embroidery needs.

  3. What materials are best suited for Schiffli embroidery?

    Schiffli embroidery works best on natural fibers like cotton and silk but can also be applied to synthetics under certain conditions.

  4. How do I place a custom design order?

    To place a custom order, contact us with your design requirements, fabric choices, and expected delivery dates, and we'll guide you through the process.

  5. Can I request revisions on my custom design?

    Yes, we allow revisions to ensure the final product meets your expectations. Specific terms are discussed during the consultation phase.

  6. What thread types and colors are available?

    We offer a wide range of thread types including rayon, polyester, and cotton, available in various colors to match your design needs.

  7. Do you offer embroidery for logos and branding purposes?

    Yes, we specialize in custom embroidery for branding, providing durable and high-quality logo embroidery for uniforms, merchandise, and more.

  8. How do I care for my embroidered fabric to ensure longevity?

    We recommend gentle washing and drying processes to maintain the integrity of the embroidery. Avoid harsh chemicals and direct heat.

  9. What are the benefits of choosing Schiffli embroidery for large-scale designs?

    Schiffli embroidery is cost-effective and efficient for large designs, making it ideal for fashion and home décor industries on a large scale.

  10. Can you recreate historic or traditional embroidery designs?

    Yes, we can accurately recreate historic and traditional designs, respecting the artistry and techniques of the originals.
