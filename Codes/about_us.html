History of Kay Kay

Kay Kay Embroideries Pvt Ltd, founded in 2000 in Navi Mumbai, is a leading embroidery business known for excellent customer service and a growing clientele. Located in the TTC Industrial Area, it offers easy access and a range of products including textile exports, kitchen appliances, and lace retail, with plans for expansion.

Kay Kay Embroidery is a renowned team of skilled artisans who specialize in exquisite embroidery and stitches. The team consists of experienced designers, skilled workers, and meticulous quality controllers. Their expertise lies in creating intricate designs, patterns, and motifs for various fabrics, including clothing, home textiles, and accessories. With a focus on precision and attention to detail, Kay Kay Embroidery produces high- quality embroidered products that are both beautiful and durable. Their dedication to excellence has earned them recognition in the industry, and their work is highly esteemed by customers worldwide.
